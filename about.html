Nature Bliss Organic Farms (NBOF) is a role model project which targets to objective of poverty alleviation in rural India by creating more business opportunities for youth and women. It touches all fundamental needs of society as given below -

अन्न (Anna)

Healthy, heirloom organic food and processed foods to rural and urban consumers.

वस्त्र (Vastra)

Healthy organic clothing and other textile items mainly for kids to take care of their delicate skin.

निवारा (Nivara)

Healthy and natural living properties & facilities like mud houses, bamboo houses etc.

‘Nature’s Bliss Organic Farms’, take care of healthy heirloom organic Anna (food) and Healthy Nivara (stay with nature) at Manbha village near to Nagpur. Total area of farm is 50 acres with 12 acres of private agroforestry, heirloom organic farm, bamboo farm, medicinal plants etc. of 150 plus different species. Second project is under development which is located 30 km from historical Burhanpur city of Madya Pradesh state under leadership of Dhiraj Pawar. It is spread across 10 acres.
